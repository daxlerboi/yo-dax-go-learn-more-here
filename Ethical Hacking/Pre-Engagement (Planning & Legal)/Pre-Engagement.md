## 0.1 Engagement Overview <a id="01-engagement-overview"></a>

* Objective of the assessment
* Type of engagement (Pentest, Red Team, Bug Bounty, Audit)
* Stakeholders & contacts
* Timeline & milestones

---

## 0.2 Legal Authorization <a id="02-legal-authorization"></a>

* Written permission / contract
* Authorization letter (Letter of Engagement)
* Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA)
* Rules of liability

---

## 0.3 Scope Definition <a id="03-scope-definition"></a>

* In-scope targets (domains, IPs, apps)
* Out-of-scope targets
* Third-party services inclusion/exclusion
* Physical vs remote scope

---

## 0.4 Rules of Engagement (RoE) <a id="04-rules-of-engagement-roe"></a>


* Allowed techniques (e.g., phishing, DoS, social engineering)
* Disallowed actions
* Testing time window (24/7 or limited hours)
* Rate limiting / safe testing constraints

---

## 0.5 Risk & Impact Assessment <a id="05-risk--impact-assessment"></a>

* Critical systems identification
* Business impact considerations
* Data sensitivity levels
* Safety precautions

---

## 0.6 Communication Plan <a id="06-communication-plan"></a>

* Primary & secondary contacts
* Escalation procedures
* Emergency stop process
* Reporting frequency (daily/weekly updates)

---

## 0.7 Compliance & Regulations <a id="07-compliance--regulations"></a>

* Applicable laws (local/international)
* Industry standards (ISO 27001, PCI-DSS, etc.)
* Data protection regulations (GDPR, etc.)

---

## 0.8 Testing Methodology Selection <a id="08-testing-methodology-selection"></a>

* Black Box / White Box / Grey Box approach
* Frameworks used (OWASP, PTES, NIST, etc.)
* Tools & techniques planned

---

## 0.9 Resource & Environment Planning <a id="09-resource--environment-planning"></a>

* Tools setup (Kali Linux, Burp Suite, Nmap, etc.)
* VPN / access credentials
* Test accounts / staging environments
* Hardware requirements

---

## 0.10 Documentation & Deliverables Planning <a id="010-documentation--deliverables-planning"></a>

* Report format
* Evidence collection method
* Screenshot/log requirements
* Final deliverables (report, risk rating, remediation plan)

---

## 0.11 Exit Criteria <a id="011-exit-criteria"></a>

* When testing is considered complete
* Conditions for stopping engagement
* Success metrics

* Or even face **legal consequences**
